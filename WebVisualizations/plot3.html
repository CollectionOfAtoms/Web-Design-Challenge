Analysis: Latitude vs. Cloudiness

At a glance this data looks to be fairly randomly distributed. Indicating clouds tend to be uniformly distributed across the globe. However, the one notably present structure is the presence of horizontal lines at 0%, 20%, 75% and 90%.

I suspect that this means that Cloudiness just has a wishy-washy definition and tends to take on values that humans find pleasing, and likely takes on values divisible by 5 because humans usually have 10 fingers. This is made to be consumed by humans after all. Note that there is no such horizontal line at 100%, because that would indicate a statement of absolute truth, and therefore should be avoided by those publishing data for the public at all costs.
